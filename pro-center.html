AFS Pro Center - Contractor Onboarding

0% complete

What you get as a Pro Center contractor

  • Instant material BOM - no manual takeoffs
  • Live AFS pricing tied to your account tier
  • Digital quote delivery + customer approval
  • Net 30 terms (after approval)
  • Dealer network pricing on qualifying volume
  • Dedicated rep + priority order fulfillment

1 Create your AFS contractor account

Sign up at amerifence.com or call your local AFS branch. You will need your business name, EIN or SSN, contractor license number, and primary billing address.

Go to AFS

2 Get your account number Required for orders

Your AFS account number (format: KAN######) is required to place orders and receive contractor pricing. You will receive it by email after your account is approved - usually within 1 business day.

3 Choose your pricing tier

AFS offers several contractor pricing tiers set by annual purchase volume: Standard (5-10% below retail), Pro Center/XLContractor (~15% below retail), and Dealer Network (best pricing + priority stock). Contact your AFS rep to confirm your tier.

4 Apply for Net 30 terms

Net 30 credit terms let you receive materials and pay within 30 days. Complete the AFS credit application with 3 trade references. Allow 3-5 business days for approval.

5 Place your first order using a BOM

Every quote generates a complete Bill of Materials with AFS SKUs and quantities. Call your AFS branch or submit via their portal. Reference your KAN account number on every order.

6 Enroll in the AFS Dealer Network

High-volume contractors (typically 50K+ annual volume) can apply for Dealer Network status: best pricing, priority fulfillment, dedicated rep, and marketing co-op support. Ask your AFS rep about eligibility.
